About Cool Runnings Fitness

Welcome to the Cool Runnings Fitness! We have a passion for runners, helping them on their journey and creating a community. CRF is all about improving the runner experience, inspiring them to stay motivated and live a healthier life.

Our Story

This website was born from the Cool Runnings Fitness app in 2023, but in reality the concept came years before.

We noticed the excitement that comes on race day. Some of that excitement comes from anticipation. While other times it comes from spectators cheering on the runners. An athlete can get a burst of energy just by hearing encouraging words from family, friends or complete strangers.

Let's be honest, running is challenging and hard to stick to because it can be lonely. Most times if you are training for a race or trying to stay in shape you are doing it alone. The question became, how to get race day encouragement during ever day runs or training was asked. The answer came in an app.

As the app was in development we wanted a place where runners could get more information about running. We also wanted a place where runners could be inspired by other runners and keep motivated. That's where the Cool Runnings Fitness came in.

We hope you will grow with us as we build this running community

Our Values

Inclusivity: We believe that running is for everyone, regardless of age, pace, or experience level. Our community welcomes runners from all walks of life.
Support: We're here to encourage and motivate each other, every step of the way.
Inspiration: We share stories, tips, and advice to help you stay motivated and inspired.
Community: We're more than just a website - we're a community of runners who support and uplift each other.
